What is Group Guidance?

Group Guidance will help you get needed opinions before you purchase anything online.

How does it work?

  • Grab this bookmarklet and put it in your browser's bookmark bar:


  • Find something you'd like to buy online and drag that item's picture or description.
With Group Guidance you first set a "brain trust" of advisers. Then, your trusted group will receive an email when you add items to your wishlist. If an adviser confirms you've made a good choice regarding your item, that adviser will approve it.
