What is Group Guidance?

Group Guidance will help you get opinions before you purchase anything online.

How does it work?

Install bookmarklet

Grab this bookmarklet and drag it into your browser's bookmark bar:

Create Group

Set a group to give you trusted opinions

Shop

Find something you'd like to buy online and add it to your pending list.

Group gets email

Each group member will receive an email when you add items. The group will approve or reject your items... majority rules.

Approved items are "unlocked"

You can purchase your approved items.
